Fried Potatoes

Description

Potatoes are a great vegetable to keep around. They are a good source of healthy carbs and many different minerals and nutrition. Many cultures use potatoes as staple and eat them at every meal.

This is a simple way to prepare fried potatoes. You may use your favorite seasoning to add unique flavor profiles or just eat them with salt. Prepared this way they are also pair nicely with fried eggs and ham.

Ingredients

  • Fresh potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the potatoes by wahsing them with cold water. You may peel them if you prefer.
  2. Add the oil to a cast iron skillet and heat on medium heat.
  3. Slice the potatoes thin and rinse them under cold water to wash off the excess starch.
  4. When the oil is about 350F, add the potatoes and fry until crispy and lightly brown.
  5. Remove from the oil, place on paper towels and sprinkle immediatly with your favorite seasoning.
