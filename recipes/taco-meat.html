Meat for Tacos

My favorite way to make beef tacos

The meat is the foundation of the taco. The only way to a world class taco is to start with world class meat. It doesn't take a lot but that is all the more reason to makes sure that the meat that you do use is very flavorful and fresh.

I use this recipe when I have taken the time to think ahead and have the time. It is a slow cooker type of recipe but I think it is absolutely superb.

Ingredients

  • 3 lb of something fatty like brisket or beef cheeks
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp salt
  • 2 tsp Cumin
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 3 cloves of fresh garlic, chopped

Steps

  1. Mix the salt, cumin and pepper in a small bowl.
  2. Remove any access moisture from the meat and coat with the olive oil. Rub the spices into the meat making sure to coat the entire surface.
  3. Place the meat in a bowl and refrigerate overnight or at least 4-5 hours.
  4. Add the onions and garlic to your meat and wrap everything in foil. Try to fold up the edges so that the juices don't leak out. You will form a kind of tinfoil package.
  5. Add the two cups of water to a crock-pot and then place the package in the crock-pot.
  6. Cook on low for around 10-11 hours. Add more water if it looks like it is running low.
  7. Remove the foil package and shred or cut the meat into small bits. Enjoy!
